About Me

I want to build things that people and businesses use. Solving problems and finding alternative answers is fun for me. I strive to find solutions that are straightforward, efficient and effective. There is a certain magical quality that simple solutions possess. Finding the balance between form and function is something that I enjoy.

I am passionately creative and resourceful while always looking to learn new skills and improve current ones. I enjoy challenges and continually growing and changing for the better. Development is exciting to me because it requires both critical and creative thinking. Each day represents a chance to harness something great, especially when considering all things tech.
